I am a biomedical engineering post-doc working in Dr. Jorge Ruas's Lab. My main focus is on exploring the interactions between adipose tissue and sensory neurons. I am particularly interested in local communication, such as the effects of sensory neuropeptides on adipocyte lipolysis and adipokine production. It is a large departure from my previous work, though connected by my fascination with tissue adaptation and remodelling. During my graduate studies, under the mentorship of Dr. Shayn Peirce-Cottler and Dr. Silvia Blemker, I created computational models to simulate muscle atrophy and regeneration. With the guidance of Dr. George Christ, my first post-doctoral work focused on skeletal muscle regeneration in hindlimb muscles. Please check out my research section for details on many of the projects I am involved in!
